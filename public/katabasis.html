What is Katabasis: A History

Katabasis is a project that has taken various forms over the years, starting originally some time in 2019 after Jace decided he wanted to make a Spirit Hunter fan game (this was back when only the one spirit hunter game even existed in the west). Originally, many of these characters, concepts and otherwise didn't exist. As of the time of publishing: (No clue when that will be), that vision has changed into its own project with its roots buried in the artistic stylings of its inspirations.
