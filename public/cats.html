Ivy

Name: Ivy

Nicknames: Mama, Little Lady, Snugglebug

Age: Unknown, estimated minimum 14

Color: I'll be honest, no clue

Likes: Tuna, snuggles, my computer chair

Dislikes: People touching her beans

When I Met Her: Near November of 2011

I met Ivy in 2011, hiding herself under my mother's car to keep warm in the cold. Over the course of the next month or so, I would regularly leave out food and sit on our back porch where she was frequently spotted and allowed her to sit in my lap when she felt comfortable. Over that month it became harder and harder to let her wander back out through the hole in our fence, and eventually I had to convince our mother to allow us to hang onto her. Once the sliding back door was opened, and she came in, I haven't let her out since.

Jamie

Name: Jamie

Nicknames: Jessie, Simon, Asshole (loving), Stinky

Age: 14

Color: Flame Point Siamese

Likes: My chicken and dumpling soup, ranch, folding himself into a pretzel

Dislikes: Loud noises, occasionally Ivy

When I Met Him: August 2023

Jamie came to me through a friend currently undergoing her vet school classes, and volunteering at the shelter in our area. Jamie was given to a friend of hers' clinic after his owner was put into a hospice facility and he could not come with. Due to his age, and being front declawed, he was scheduled to be euthanized if not picked up that week. I absolutely begged my friend to drive me the two hour round trip to bring the little guy home. He perches like a gargoyle when he cuddles (ow), but he's nonetheless one of the most affectionate little guys I've had the pleasure to know since then.
