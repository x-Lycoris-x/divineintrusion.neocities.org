I find myself at a strange choke point in regards to moving. I've been telling myself to trust my instincts, but they often are difficult to parse from anxiety. I tell myself I'll listen to my gut on what's wrong, but I'm having a hard time parsing if there's anything wrong or if I'm just afraid. Is there some reason?
I feel stuck and unable to accept what I know is a good opportunity because something about it is making me afraid.

November 16th, 2025

Today was a good day, at least in some kind of theory. I had friendsgiving and sat down to make green bean casserole. I don't cook for shit, so honestly I was really scared I'd end up fucking it up. The whole process was fucking hellish. I burned the shit out of the garlic, burned my fuckin hands with the canola oil, god it felt like a fucking disaster from start to finish. Apparently it was actually pretty good though, aside from being a bit under salted. Honestly, for all that stress, that was the funny part of it all. For all the things I was worried being wrong, it was the salt that made an otherwise shockingly perfect dish a little underwhelming. Makes sense, I erred on the side of caution with the salt and under-did it, cause I can't taste salt all that well unless it's in stupidly high doses, and I know I tend to oversalt my food. Big shocker, the one time I deliberately salt less than I would, it's under salted.

There is a lot of lingering...issues, about the party beside the food. Honestly, I feel so out of place around my friends. I don't really even know if they'd still be friends with me if they thought about it for very long. I feel like I just have the greatest propensity to fuck everything up. I don't imagine it is that way, my brain is honestly probably making the worst out a thing that wasn't as bad as I think it was. Every joke that didn't get a laugh, every time I lingered on a topic too long, every time I was too loud or not loud enough. I feel like I'm nobody's best friend, and like. I don't want it to affect me as much as it does, but god damn does it.

I feel like I just want to run away from people, I want to disappear from their lives. It's drastic, and I don't imagine it would be all that smart. I guess there's a part of my brain that kind of wants to test the idea. Do people really give a shit, do I mean anything to anyone? Would people miss me or even notice if I managed to disappear. I don't think I would be able to accept if anyone reacted though, and honestly it's just an exercise in self harm to think that way. I know I need to be better than that. I wish it wasn't so easy to get drowned by the thought of it all. I wish I didn't come home from trying to have fun feeling like a fucking loser who can't do anything right and wanting to sob and never be around my friends again. They mean a lot to me. I just don't imagine I mean the same thing to any of them.

I'm kind of worried that if they had thought about it, or others didn't keep trying to put faith in me and take me along to things, that people would stop inviting me to places just to avoid the inconvenience. I kind of want to cry again, so I'm going to hang up the proverbial phone for now.
