Who are these people? (Preface)

Admittedly, I'm incredibly unfamiliar with the specific history of Fantrials in the ARG space. This page will be more so a set of shrines to the rabbits I've played in fantrials over the years, since they have a special place in my heart (one of them being directly linked to how I met my partner, Bio).
In the future, I would love to do a dive on the specific history of ARGs and fantrials, but that's an undertaking for a different day beyond the somewhat drama filled bare minimum history.

From here onward, unless something changes, I won't be participating in any more fantrials to focus on other projects.

Fun fact, I'm also one of the co runners of the Fantrials Fuckshit tumblr (Mod Rose)! There's some wild shit out there, and I welcome you to read up on the stories people have submitted!

〖#088 | The Phoenix Trials〗

Real Name: Nicholi Bauer

Teams (If Applicable): Netherland Dwarves (Member, Elected Leader), Angora (Post Dwarves Eradication)

Status Post Trial: Alive, in Hiding

Media

Rabbit Overview: Nicholi began as a fan oc for the Spirit Hunter (then only known as Death Mark) franchise. In my spare time during my senior year of high school, I was either obsessing over the series or using any of the many OCs I made for the game in other environments. I had very few irl friends during that time due to moving to Texas in my senior year against my wishes, so it became my primary way of connecting with others online through trial spaces or the Death Mark Discord I was heavily a part of at the time. This rabbit holds a special place in my heart, since through his (now defunct) tumblr, a very special ARG found its way to me and led to my current state of affairs. I hold his trial singularly responsible for allowing me to escape an incredibly abusive home situation and lead a proper adult life due to the connections I made in this community. This trial, sadly, ended without much fanfare after the runner experienced massive burnout and ended the project early.
