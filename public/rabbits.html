What the Hell is a Fan Trial?

Currently, my knowledge of the history of the community is based primarily in its later years. However, at least for now, I will leave a link to a fun reddit thread I found detailing a lot of the admittedly drama filled bare minimum history.

In the future, I do plan on doing a deep dive on the subject, and writing my own essay on the matter. I hope you'll look forward to that with me!

This page will stay at its most up to date state, as I will not continue to participate in other trials in order to focus on other projects.

Fun fact, I also co run a tumblr blog dedicated to some of the more...odd incidents in fan trials: Fantrials Fuckshit ! I'm mod rose! I welcome you to read up on some of the crazier stories people have submitted!

Who Are These People?

And why are they cool as fuck?

Below this point, you'll find the various OCs that I've used as my mask in various projects calling themselves fan trials over the years. Each and every one is important to both my history, and development as a writer.

One rule I had, and continue to have, is that unless the project ended early on or I became uninterested in adding the trial to the "rabbit's" lore, once I had played a character in a trial, they would be "Retired" and become unable to be played again. This was partially to prevent creating a type of character which was (and remains) controversial in the space: Rabbits who incomprehensibly survived multiple trials.

Thus, I set out to give them their own little burrow to retire to on my website in honor of their "service".

Each and every one of these characters has at one point in time been representative of me as a person, and my window into the world of trials.

〖 ██████ | No Number | Seeking Redemption 〗

The Trial's Story

This story was a rather unconventional start to my experience with fantrials. Rather than a traditional Habit Fantrial, these were framed as a sort of murder mystery, shortly after the release of Who Killed Markiplier.

The Trials themselves were run by Darkiplier, and Antisepticeye, though I don't recall what they called themselves as their fake names.

My memory of some of the specifics is admitedly hazy, but it is the only trial I have participated in which (nearly) reached its intended conclusion. While the ending event was being prepared, apparently long forming schisms within the mod team had come to a head.

Instead of the ending event that was planned, we all recieved a sudden message about the cancelation of the event, and the end of the project rather abruptly. With sudden access to the entire discord server and no more mod team, a lot of us were left to write our own endings with the knowledge of what could have been.

Teams (If Applicable): Arcana (Initial), Wildvernack (Shuffle), Korsakoff (Post Merge)

Status Post Trial: Alive, but Restrung

No surviving media from her trials exists :(

Character Overview: █████ was the first rabbit to enter the burrow at the age of 17. She is an incredibly poorly idealized version of our 16/17 year old self's idea of self actualization. It was an unfortunate byproduct of the abuse we were suffering at the hands of our immediate family back then. While she does bear our deadname, we've taken to calling her Renée in order to have a name to refer to her by (and also as a reference to another project we put her in).

The character herself was 16 at the start of the trials. She joined as a side hobby, something to keep herself entertained during her long shifts working at a fast food place. She was still attending high school as well, though later in the story she would end up dropping out involuntarily when her mother no longer assisted her in reaching campus, and the walk was not feasible (over 4 hours one way). It was reflective of our real life situation at the time, though we did graduate high school thankfully.

She became incredibly attatched to the members of her original team, Arcana, and later the members of Wildvernack when teams were restructured. She tried to be a friend to all, or at least well liked by most, with a heavy emphasis on her need to be of service to others and a protective nature of those she considered family.

██████ was the eldest of three siblings, and made her better attempts to keep them at length from the trial proceedings the more dangerous matters became. This number was actually less than our own real siblings. We chose to keep her as the elder sister to two brothers, both for the sake of privacy, and simplicity.

The way she viewed the game was simply, a game (until it wasn't). In her mind, she had been invited to participate in a digital murder mystery, to solve puzzles and collect clues with other members of her team. This would later come to be challenged as the game masters (playing Darkiplier and Antisepticeye) began causing harm of a physical and psychological nature to the members of her team "family".

Some elements of the fantrials/slenderverse trappings were most evident in her "Restringing". A mechanic which closely resembled the idea of a Proxy, ██████ was eventually restrung after a confrontation on behalf of another player against Antisepticeye. All over a silly meme :). A Restringing's nature depended on the individual, but in essence served to make the subject a compliant servant of the two entities running the game. For ██████, it took the form of one last desperate flight into the woods behind her home before returning a bitterly compliant person who refused to make jokes. She would comply with orders, but not without outwardly expressing her distaste for them in a now much more subdued manner than her previously bubbly personality.

She, unlike many others who find themselves involved in trials, actually survived the end of her time! When the game masters had no more use for their puppets at the game's end, she was left discarded within the now abandoned home she had once shared with her biological family. The game for her ended with a few of her friends from her previous teams promising to find her and travel the country together. Isn't it sweet? : )

〖#088 | The Phoenix Trials〗

Real Name: Nicholi Bauer

Teams (If Applicable): Netherland Dwarves (Member, Elected Leader), Angora (Post Dwarves Eradication)

Status Post Trial: Alive, in Hiding

Media

Rabbit Overview: Nicholi began as a fan oc for the Spirit Hunter (then only known as Death Mark) franchise. In my spare time during my senior year of high school, I was either obsessing over the series or using any of the many OCs I made for the game in other environments. I had very few irl friends during that time due to moving to Texas in my senior year against my wishes, so it became my primary way of connecting with others online through trial spaces or the Death Mark Discord I was heavily a part of at the time. This rabbit holds a special place in my heart, since through his (now defunct) tumblr, a very special ARG found its way to me and led to my current state of affairs. I hold his trial singularly responsible for allowing me to escape an incredibly abusive home situation and lead a proper adult life due to the connections I made in this community. This trial, sadly, ended without much fanfare after the runner experienced massive burnout and ended the project early.
