What is Spirit Hunter?

Spirit Hunter is a horror adventure series developed and published by Experience Inc. beginning with the first game: Death Mark in June of 2017 for the PS Vita in Japan. The series explores a primarily visual novel adventure, as one clears the various stages by investigating a rumor regarding a spirit, and then eliminating said spirit (either by saving, or destroying them). Following its worldwide port to various systems via a partnership with Aksys Games, the sequels: NG (No Good) and Death Mark II were also slated for translation and a western release after 2018.

Rules

The following are the rules for this webring. Please read them carefully and decide if this webring is for you!
  1. You are 18 years old or older
    2. Why?
    The Spirit Hunter series is a game which contains mature themes/content, and I do not feel that as much as I love this series, that minors should be shown it.
    Moreover, as adult/mature sites can be listed in this directory, I would not be comfortable listing minors alongside sites containing mature/adult content. Adults have a responsibility to look out for the minors in our communities by creating barriers to entry for vulnerable parties.
    Feel free to come back when you're older if you don't meet the criteria now!
  2. You're a fan of any of the entries in the Spirit Hunter series!
  3. You have your own website that can display the webring widget (carrds or linktrees won't work I'm afraid, sorry!)
  4. NSFW/Mature sites are accepted, but all content must be labeled or warned for before being able to access. It should not be the first thing I see on your website.
  5. Hate speech and bigotry have no place here, and if found on your site, you will not be invited to join.
