Website Manifesto

In all frankness, I barely understand the base concept of a site manifesto.
A reason to make something, a reason for a thing to exist. A document containing the manifest hopes and dreams riding on a project. A statement of intent.
A lot of the reasons listed here, to make this website, are incredibly personal. They will not resonate with everyone hwo reads them, but they truly don't have to.
I simply keep mine here to view, for posterity to myself and hopefully a source of inspiration for others.
I think if you want to do something like this, or hell just start any kind of long term project, it's always important to examine your reasons for starting things.
It can be easy for me to lose my way sometimes, and I hope to leave this behind as a little crumb trail to bring me home on the days where it's hard to continue things.
As much as I want to have a lot to say and make some deep, introspective essay about it, I honestly will probably keep to simpler terms if only to get my point out there.

Proof of Will

Over the years, I've had the desire to start quite a few creative projects. The key word in that statement is desire. Despite that fact, I have an incredibly difficult time taking those first steps when I'm unsure of my own ability to follow through.
In some regard, there's probably some undiagnosed shit that's making it more difficult, but I cannot and will not have that be the crux of my inability to do something.
It provides me a larger confident boost to work on things in the future when I can link back to the tangible products of my work. This will be one such example to me, something I can physically interact with as proof to myself that I can do something that requires time, immense effort, and to gain a level of mastery over a new skill or medium.

Spite

Over the years, I've found that one of the most effective motivators for me to work on things that I may have difficulty with, is pure unadulterated spite. That and stress, but I'm not about to make a thing that I'm supposed to enjoy a source of suffering.
When I feel like I'm not only giving myself joy, but actively giving the middle finger to someone or something else in my way? Mwah~, chef's kiss~
Here's to me working through my self esteem issues via the power of html, css, and javascript.

A need to overshare all the time forever

So, unsurprising to anyone who knows me, I have a deeply rooted thing with oversharing/sharing at volumes that are frankly probably a little overwhelming when I'm excited about something.
In that regard, this is kind of a quiet place for me to do that, where I can make entire spaces dedicated to things I'd love to go on a tangent about.
On one level, its giving it a place to flow. On the other level, it's giving me essentially the equivalent of physical presentation materials for when I want to show something something I like or that continues to eat at my mind like a worm.
This site is my worms, please enjoy them as they wriggle in the earth and keep me awake at night.
