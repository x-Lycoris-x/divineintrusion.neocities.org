FAQ

Software?: I draw on Clip Studio Paint (PC) and just pen and paper irl. Absolutely Cannot draw on mobile for shit, I've tried XD

Hardware?: Gaomon Display Tablet, Model PD1161

Can I Use?: My art is free to be used explicitly for personal use (i.e. Profile Photos) when appropriate credit is given. My art is not to be used for monetary gain of any kind. If you're looking to commission artwork for monetary use, please see below. My art is not to be used to train AI. I will sue, fuck around and find out.

Can I (re-)post?: You're welcome to reach out via the Guestbook, or other socials linked and available. All requests are subject to approval, and must be posted with direct credit and a link back to me. Thank you for understanding!

Commissions/Requests?: Currently, no, but I may consider them in the future.
